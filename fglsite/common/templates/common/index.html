Welcome to FGL



What is a FGL?

Fantasy Gambling League is a game conceived by an uncle of Liam Woulfe and adopted by the ex-residents of Calton Walk. Each week, the Commissioner will publish odds on a group of Premier League games. Players are given a 'fantasy' allowance to bet on those games as they see fit. Once results are in, winnings are calculated and the league table is updated.



How do I play?

You'll first need an account. Only way to register at the moment is to ask Ollie to get you set up.



What is a Commissioner?

The Commissioner is responsible for creating and maintaining the season. Season creation can be actioned using the link below. Once a season has been created and players have joined, the Commissioner may begin populating gameweek odds.
